Formatter: Textile Plus
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The "Textile Plus" formatter combines Textile with some custom processing to
prevent widowed words and wrap text features for styling with CSS.

Version: 1.0.1
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 30 April 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Upload the 'textileplusformatter' folder in this archive to your Symphony
	'extensions' folder.

2.  Enable it by selecting the "Formatter: Textile Plus", choose Enable from the
	with-selected menu, then click Apply.

4.	You can now choose "Textile Plus" as a text formatter.
